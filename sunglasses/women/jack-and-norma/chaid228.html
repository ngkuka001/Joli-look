Product Information

A Beautiful Mess is an award-winning lifestyle blog run by sisters, Elsie and Emma. In collaboration with BonLook, they have designed a hip square frame named after their grandparents, Jack & Norma. Quality & Manufacturing

Our Manufacturing Process

All of our frames are designed in house in Montreal.

The lenses are fitted to your prescription by a certified optician at a top Essilor North American lab.

We perform rigorous individual and manual quality control on every pair.

Our frames are handmade using superior materials such as high quality cellulose acetate sheets and hypoallergenic stainless steel.

Our lenses

Anti-scratch and anti-glare coatings included.

100% UV protection included.

All our prescription lenses are from Essilor, the world’s leading manufacturer of first-rate ophthalmic lenses.
